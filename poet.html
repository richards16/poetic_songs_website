HERE SOME TIPS TO BECOME A POETR!

* Read poetry from various genres, time periods, and cultures to understand different styles and techniques. Familiarize yourself with classic poets and their works to gain insight into the foundational elements of poetry. Keep a journal to jot down thoughts, observations, and ideas that can be turned into poems.Make a habit of writing poetry daily, even if it’s just a few lines. Consistency helps improve your skills.

* Experiment with different poetic forms (sonnets, haikus, free verse, etc.) to find what resonates with you.Don’t be afraid to play with words, rhythms, and sounds to create unique expressions. Draw inspiration from nature, your surroundings, and everyday experiences.Reflect on your emotions, memories, and personal experiences as sources of poetic material.
